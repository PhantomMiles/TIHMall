Our History

ICC-TIH Mall was conceived with a vision to revolutionize the shopping landscape by integrating cutting-edge technology and seamless services. Our journey began in 2024, marking our first major appearance at the Enugu Tech Fest. This event provided the perfect platform to showcase our innovative approach and connect with a vibrant tech community.

Since then, ICC-TIH Mall has continued to grow, driven by our commitment to delivering tomorrow’s solutions today. We have expanded our offerings, partnered with leading brands, and introduced smart retail experiences that set us apart in the industry.

Our team is passionate about creating a vibrant, inclusive, and forward-thinking environment for shoppers and businesses alike. As we look to the future, we remain dedicated to innovation, customer satisfaction, and making a positive impact in our community.

Our Mission

At ICC-TIH Mall, our mission is to empower communities by providing innovative, technology-driven retail and service experiences. We strive to bridge the gap between tomorrow’s possibilities and today's needs, ensuring every customer enjoys convenience, quality, and a sense of belonging.

We are dedicated to fostering a dynamic environment where creativity, inclusivity, and customer satisfaction are at the heart of everything we do. By leveraging smart solutions and strategic partnerships, we aim to set new standards in the retail industry.

Our commitment extends beyond commerce—we believe in making a positive impact on society, supporting local businesses, and nurturing sustainable growth for all stakeholders.
